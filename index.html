About Anemia

Anemia is a condition marked by a deficiency of red blood cells or hemoglobin.

Causes:

  • Iron or Vitamin B12 Deficiency
  • Chronic diseases
  • Genetic conditions like Thalassemia

Symptoms:

  • Fatigue and Weakness
  • Pale Skin
  • Shortness of breath
  • Dizziness
